View and Filter all QuotesÂ¶

To view all Quotes, navigate to Sales > Quotes in the main menu.

A quote in OroCommerce is used to negotiate with the customer (e.g. offer better price, more convenient quantities and additional services). A quote may be created in response to a customer request for quote, or as a result of the direct communication with the customer. Once the customer is happy with the offer in the quote and is ready to proceed with their order, they accept the quote.

Note

To handle big volume of data, use page switcher, increase View Per Page or use filters to narrow down the list to the codes you need.

The following information about the Quotes is available:

  • Quote #
  • Owner
  • Customer User
  • Customer
  • Internal Status
  • Locked
  • Expired
  • Valid Until
  • PO Number
  • DNSLT
  • Create At
  • Update At

Next steps

You can perform the following actions with every item of the Quotes list:

  • View a Quote details: Click on the item to open its details page.

    OR

    Alternatively: Hover over the more actions menu to the right of the item and click to open its details page.

  • Edit a Quote details: Hover over the more actions menu to the right of the item and click to start editing its details.

  • Expire a Quote: Click to the right of the item to start editing its details.

  • Delete a Quote: Hover over the more actions menu to the right of the item and click to remove the Quote.

You can perform the following actions on the Quotes page:

  • Create Quote
  • View and Filter all Quotes
