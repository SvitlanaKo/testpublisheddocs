To view all Quotes, navigate to Sales > Quotes in the main menu.

A quote in OroCommerce is used to negotiate with the customer (e.g. offer better price, more convenient quantities and additional services). A quote may be created in response to a customer request for quote, or as a result of the direct communication with the customer. Once the customer is happy with the offer in the quote and is ready to proceed with their order, they accept the quote.

Next steps

You can perform the following actions with every item of the Quotes list:

  • View a Quote details: Click on the item to open its details page.

    OR

    Alternatively: Hover over the More Options menu to the right of the item and click to open its details page.

  • Edit a Quote details: Hover over the More Options menu to the right of the item and click to start editing its details.

  • Expire a Quote: Click to the right of the item to start editing its details.

  • Delete a Quote: Hover over the More Options menu to the right of the item and click to remove the Quote.
