Editing a Tax Jurisdiction¶

To edit the Tax Jurisdiction details:

  1. Navigate to Taxes > Tax Jurisdictions in the main menu.
  2. Hower over the More Options menu to the right of the item and click the to start editing its details. The following page opens:
  1. Update the Code, Description, and the covered adresses includign the zip codes to modify the Tax Jurisdiction.
  2. Click Save on the top right of the page.

The Tax Jurisdiction is updated.

Association of the Tax Jurisdiction with the Tax Rule¶

You can edit the association of the Tax Jurisdiction with other tax components when editing the tax rule details (see the respective topic for more information).
