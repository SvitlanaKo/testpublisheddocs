Edit an OrderÂ¶

To edit an order:

  1. Navigate to Sales > Orders in the main menu.

  2. Choose an order in the list, hover over the More Options menu to the right of the item and click to start editing its details.

    Alternatively, click the order to open its details. On the order details page, click the Edit button on the top right.

  3. Update the required information. See Create an Order from Scratch and Manage Promotions in Orders topics for detailed information on the available options.

  4. Click Save on the top right of the page.

The order is updated.
