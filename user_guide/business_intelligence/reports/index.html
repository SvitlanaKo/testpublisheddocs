ReportsÂ¶

In OroCommerce and OroCRM, you can create reports based on the details of any entity available in the system.

You can get easy-to-understand adjustable reports on the budgets and activity of different customers, on statuses of carts and the current state of actual and potential sales, on the performance of your marketing effort, specifics of recent activities, etc.

There is a number of out-of-the-box reports, such as Leads by Date and Opportunities by Status, but you can also create your own custom reports.

System reports are read-only. You can view the report output and filter the resulting data.
