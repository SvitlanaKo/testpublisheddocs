Comment on a Task¶

  • From the Task View Page
  • From the Related Entity View Page

From the Task View Page¶

To comment on a task when viewing the task details:

  1. In the main menu, navigate to Activities>Tasks.

  2. On the task list, click the required task.

    Alternatively, you can click the More Options menu at the end of the corresponding row and then click the View icon.

  3. On the task view page, click Comments.

  4. In the Comments section, click the Add Comment button in the lower-right corner.

  5. In the Add Comment dialog, enter your comment. If rich text functionality is enabled for you, you can format your comment in the editor. You can also add an attachment to your comment.

  6. Click the Add button.

    Your comment appears in the Comments section of the task view page.

Tip

You can edit or remove your comment: find your comment and click the More Options menu at the end of the corresponding row. Then click the Edit / Delete icon.

From the Related Entity View Page¶

Entity records that are specified in the task context have this task displayed in the Activity sections of their view pages.

You can add a comment to a task directly from the view page of a related entity.

  1. On the entity record view page, click Activity.

  2. In the Activity section, choose a task to comment, and click it to see details.

  3. Click the Add Comment button in the lower-right corner.

  4. In the Add Comment dialog, enter your comment. If rich text functionality is enabled for you, you can format your comment in the editor. You can also add an attachment to your comment.

  5. Click the Add button.

    Your comment appears in the Comments section of the task details.
